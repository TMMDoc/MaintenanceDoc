Daily maintenance

This chapter lists all the available daily maintenance tasks for the JFY_BFC Laser cutting machine. Daily maintenance tasks are to be performed every 8 hours of machine operation.

Each task includes maintenance personnel to perform the task, maintenance interval, required tools and materials, and step-by-step instructions to perform the maintenance.
