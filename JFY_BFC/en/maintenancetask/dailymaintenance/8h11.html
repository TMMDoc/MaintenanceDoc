Checking the safety light barrier

Trained Maintenance Personnel

Maintenance interval

8 operating hours

Check the integrity and functionality of the safety light barrier once per shift and after a collision.

Condition

  • The machine is switched on.

Procedure

Check the integrity of the safety light barrier

  1. Check all components of the safety light barrier (mirror post, transmitter and receiver) for damage.

  2. If there is damage:

    • Do not use the machine.

    • Inform Technical Service.

Check the functionality of the safety light barrier

  1. Before the check, ensure that the safety light barrier has been acknowledged.

  2. Interrupt the safety light barrier.

    • SAFETY LIGHT BARRIER INTERRUPTED flashes on the operating panel and start post.

    • Feed hold is triggered.

  1. If the safety light barrier cannot be acknowledged or if no reaction occurs on interruption:

    • Do not use machine.

    • Inform Technical Service.
