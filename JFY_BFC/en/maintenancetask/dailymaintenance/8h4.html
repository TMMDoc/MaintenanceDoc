Clean the machine and working area

Machine Operator

Maintenance interval

8 operating hours

The work area inside and outside the machine must be cleaned regularly.

Means, Tools, Materials

  • Breathing mask safety class FFP3 (or equivalent).

  • Industrial vacuum cleaner "Dust class M" or "Dust class H" in accordance with IEC/EN 60335-2-69 or similar.

Do not use any solvents or compressed air for cleaning.

Health hazard due to inhaling dust!

  • Breathing mask of safety class FFP3 (or similar) must be worn.

  • To vacuum dust: use industrial vacuum cleaner "Dust class M" or "Dust class H" in accordance with IEC/ EN 60335-2-69 or similar.

Procedure

  1. Turn the SAFETY-MODE key switch to position 1, remove the key and stow it away safely.

  2. Remove coarse soiling and waste parts from the work area.

  3. Remove the slag.

  4. Vacuum the machine and the work area with an industrial vacuum cleaner.
