Check and clean the bellows on the X, Y and Z axes

Check and clean the bellows on the X, Y and Z axes

Trained Maintenance Personnel

Maintenance interval

2000 operating hours

Means, Tools, Materials

  • Industrial vacuum cleaner "Dust class M" or "Dust class H" in accordance with IEC/EN 60335-2-69 or similar.

Procedure

  1. Check the bellows for damage such as cracks or holes, and for stability.

  2. Remove the dust from the bellows with an industrial vacuum cleaner.

  3. If the protective cover is seriously damaged, please inform JFY’s maintenance service department.
