Clean the guideway of the X, Y axes

Clean the guideway of the X, Y axes

Trained Maintenance Personnel

Maintenance interval

2,000 operating hours

The sliders on the X, Y axes, and the guide rail must be inspected and cleaned.

Condition

  • Move the Z-axis and Y-axis to the mid-stroke position.

  • Switch off the machine in the correct way, switch off the MAIN SWITCH, lock it, and remove the key.

Means, Tools, Materials

  1. Clean cloth.

  2. WD-40 cleaner

Procedure

Risk of injury caused by axes moving at uncontrolled speed!

In the event of an error, the linear axes may move at uncontrolled speeds against the fixed stops.

  • Switch off the machine during the cleaning work.

  1. Detach the bellows on the left Y axis.

  2. Push the bellows to the left to expose the Y guide.

  3. Spray the cleaner onto the guide rails.

  4. Thoroughly clean the Y guide in the open area of the bellows by gently wiping with a clean cloth.

  5. Check if the sliders on the guide rails are damaged and replace, if necessary.

  6. Reinstall the bellows.

  7. Detach the bellows of the right Y guiding element.

  8. Push the bellows to the right to expose the Y guide.

  9. Spray the cleaner onto the guide rails.

  10. Thoroughly clean the Y guide in the open area of the bellows with a clean cloth.

  11. Check if the sliders on the guide rails are damaged and replace it if necessary.

  12. Reinstall the bellows.

  13. The same method applies to the X axes.

  14. If the guide rails or sliders are damaged, please notify the JFY Technical Service.
