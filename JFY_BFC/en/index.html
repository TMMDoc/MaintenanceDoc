  • Maintenance tasks
    • Daily maintenance
      • Check or clean spark arrester
      • Check the pressure reducer and compressed air supply
      • Cleaning the internal exhaust module
      • Check safety cabin and laser protection window
      • Clean the machine and working area
      • Check the proximity switch at pallet gate (option)
      • Cleaning toothed slats and support, and replacing them if necessary
      • Check the pallet for damage
      • Cleaning the ceramic part
      • Cleaning and, if necessary, replacing the cutting nozzle and lateral air blast nozzle
      • Empty the scrap bin and clean the loading area
      • Checking the safety light barrier
    • Customer maintenance
      • Check the dust collection box
      • Check the filter mat and cleaning/replacing if necessary
      • Check and clean the laser filter mat
      • Replacing the air filter of the electrical cabinet fan
      • Change the cooling water
      • Lubricating the Z axis guide carriage
      • Testing and cleaning the cable carriers on the X, Y and Z axes
      • Check the safety door lock of the protective door (option)
      • Clean the guideway of the X, Y axes
      • Cleaning the racks on the X and Y axes
      • Check and clean the bellows on the X, Y and Z axes
      • Checking the lubrication line for air pockets, and exhaust if necessary
      • Testing the input controller for operating pressure
      • Check the indicator light of the machine
      • Cleaning the chain of the pallet transport unit
      • Check the track rollers and brushes at the pallet
      • Cleaning the electrical cabinet
      • Testing EMERGENCY STOP push-button
      • Replace the filter cartridge
